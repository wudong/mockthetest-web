Life in the UK Test

What is it?

Everyone seeking settlement in the UK need to pass the life in the uk test. The test is a computer based test, lasts 45 minutes, with a total of 24 questions, need at least 18 (75%) correct to pass. It consists of only single-choice and two-choice questions.

The test topic includes history, culture, the monarch, and government with most people found history the most difficult part. Do you know that how many wife Herry VII have and where are they coming from?

Bear in mind that not many native Britons can pass without preparing ; the test is not cheap either with a fee of £50! Challenge the claim if you feel confident.
